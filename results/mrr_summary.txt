Mean Reciprocal Rank (MRR) measures the average rank position 
of the correct movie in search results.

Higher MRR = Better (correct movie appears earlier)
  1.0 = Perfect (always rank 1)
  0.5 = Good (avg rank 2)
  0.33 = Okay (avg rank 3)
